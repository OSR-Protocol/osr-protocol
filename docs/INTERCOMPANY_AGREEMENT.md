## RECITALS

A. Licensor owns and has developed proprietary software technology, including but not limited to the System R AI trading operating system, comprising compute infrastructure, language model integration, risk management systems, market connectivity, and related operational tooling (the "Technology").

B. Licensee is a British Virgin Islands company formed for the purpose of issuing and managing the $OSR utility token on the Solana blockchain, which meters access to compute infrastructure.

C. Licensee wishes to license certain Technology from Licensor to enable the platform operations accessible through $OSR compute credits.

D. The parties wish to document their relationship to ensure clarity regarding intellectual property ownership and operational responsibilities.

---

## AGREEMENT

### 1. Grant of License

1.1. Licensor hereby grants to Licensee a non-exclusive, non-transferable, worldwide license to use the Technology solely for the purpose of operating the OSR Protocol platform and providing compute infrastructure services to $OSR token holders and stablecoin users.

1.2. The license does not transfer ownership of any intellectual property from Licensor to Licensee. All intellectual property rights in and to the Technology remain with Licensor.

1.3. Licensee shall not sublicense, assign, or transfer this license without the prior written consent of Licensor.

### 2. Intellectual Property Ownership

2.1. The Technology, including all source code, algorithms, architectures, documentation, and related intellectual property, is and shall remain the sole property of Licensor.

2.2. Licensee does not own, develop, or hold any intellectual property through this Agreement. Licensee is a token issuance and protocol management entity.

2.3. Any improvements, modifications, or derivatives of the Technology created during the term of this Agreement shall be the property of Licensor.

### 3. License Fees

3.1. In consideration of the license granted herein, Licensee shall pay Licensor a royalty of [PERCENTAGE]% of gross platform revenue received by Licensee from $OSR compute credit consumption and stablecoin service payments.

3.2. Royalty payments shall be made monthly in USDC to an account designated by Licensor.

3.3. Licensee shall provide Licensor with monthly revenue reports detailing gross platform revenue, credit consumption, and royalty calculations.

### 4. Operational Separation

4.1. Licensor operates the SaaS platform, manages fiat billing relationships, and provides technology services from its Florida operations.

4.2. Licensee operates the token economy, manages $OSR token issuance, presale, treasury operations, and on-chain governance from its BVI operations.

4.3. Each party is responsible for its own regulatory compliance in its respective jurisdiction.

4.4. Neither party is an agent, partner, or joint venturer of the other. Neither party has authority to bind the other.

### 5. Term and Termination

5.1. This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for an initial term of five (5) years.

5.2. Either party may terminate this Agreement with twelve (12) months written notice.

5.3. Upon termination, Licensee shall cease use of the Technology within ninety (90) days and return or destroy all confidential materials.

### 6. Representations and Warranties

6.1. Licensor represents that it has the right and authority to grant the license described herein.

6.2. Licensee represents that it is duly incorporated in the British Virgin Islands and has the authority to enter into this Agreement.

### 7. Governing Law

7.1. This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of Florida, United States.

7.2. Any disputes arising under this Agreement shall be resolved through binding arbitration in Miami, Florida.

### 8. Entire Agreement

8.1. This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties regarding the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, and agreements.
